Tunisian startup WildyNess raises pre-seed round

Tunisian travel-tech startup WildyNess has closed its pre-seed round in a co-lead by Bridging Angels and African Diaspora Network, for an undisclosed amount.

Editorial Team
3 November, 2025 1 minute read

Tunisian travel-tech startup WildyNess closed its pre-seed round in a co-lead from Bridging Angels and the African Diaspora Network, for an undisclosed amount.

The new funding will be used for regional expansion into Saudi Arabia, Algeria, Oman, and the UAE, as well as developing the platform's technical infrastructure and improving the user experience.

You might be interested | Roadmap to securing venture capital

Founded in 2021 by Achraf El Ouadi and Rim Bourguiba, WildyNess aims to connect travelers seeking authentic experiences with local entrepreneurs in the tourism sector, through experiences designed in collaboration with these small-scale entrepreneurs under the concept of community-based tourism.

This investment marks the first funding round of its kind in the North African travel sector, reflecting international investor interest in sustainable business models beyond fintech, which typically captures the largest share of regional funding.
